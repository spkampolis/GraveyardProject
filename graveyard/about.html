Για τα Κοιμητήρια του Δήμου Φυλής

Founded in 1904, Κοιμητήρια Δήμου Φυλής has served as a resting place for generations of families. Our mission is to provide a serene and respectful environment for loved ones. With over 100 years of history, our cemetery remains a vital part of the local community.
